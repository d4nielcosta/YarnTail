How To Read Your Favourite Patterns

Pattern reading instructions should appear here.

Hi, I see you are worried about how to share your first patter on YarnTail.
No need! We made it easy for you.

Unlike other knitting websites, YarnTail does not limit you to photos and typed instructions in order to represent knitting patterns.

Pattern input is performed through an easy to use interface that represents your patterns as a graph. Cool, right?
For instructions on how to upload new patterns can be found in Upload Instructions.
The pattern is then taken and converted into data that we can store on our servers.

When you want to look at a pattern, we take that data and reconstruct the graphic representation for you to view.
