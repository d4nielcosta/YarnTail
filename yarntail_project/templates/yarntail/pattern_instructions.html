How To Read Your Favourite Patterns

Pattern reading instructions should appear here.

Unlike other knitting websites, YarnTail does not limit you to photos and typed instructions in order to represent knitting patterns. Pattern input is performed through an easy to use interface that represents your patterns as a graph. This is then taken and converted into data that we can store on our servers. When you wants to look at a pattern, we take that data and reconstruct the graphic representation for you to view.
