What Is YarnTail?

Confused? Your in the right place! YarnTail is a website for people who knit, and want to browse and share knitting
patterns on the internet.

Unlike other knitting websites, YarnTail does not limit you to photos and typed instructions in order to
represent knitting patterns.

Pattern input is performed through an easy to use interface that represents your patterns as a graph. Cool, right?
(Instructions on how to upload new patterns can be found here, by the way.)
The pattern is then taken and converted into data that we can store on our servers.

When you want to look at a pattern, we take that data (knits, purls, colored yarn) and reconstruct the graph
