Upload Pattern Instructions

Hi, I see you are worried about how to share your first pattern on YarnTail.
No need! We made it easy for you.

Just login into our website and you should now be able to see a "add new pattern" on to top-right corner of the screen.
You'll find you have two different ways of input.

The first is a graphical representation. This is what YarnTail is all about. You should give it a go!
Further instructions on how to use the graph can be found in the instructions box next to it.

If you don't feel confident using the graph just yet, you can switch for the second tab and input your pattern in the good old textbox.


If you experience any difficulties please drop us a message at yarntail@mail.com



Best Regards,
The YarnTail Community
